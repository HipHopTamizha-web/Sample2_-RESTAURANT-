Our Story

Where Tradition Meets the
Urban Palate

"Born from the streets, refined for the soul."

Urban Tandoor Collective was born in 2017 from a simple idea: what if Delhi's legendary street food met world-class culinary techniques? Nestled in the vibrant lanes of Hauz Khas Village, our restaurant has become a landmark for food enthusiasts seeking something beyond ordinary.

Our chefs blend traditional Indian spices with global cooking methods — think truffle-infused butter chicken, wasabi tikka, and deconstructed chaat. Every dish tells a story of innovation rooted in heritage.
